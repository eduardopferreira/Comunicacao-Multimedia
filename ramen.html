Ponyo's Ramen


Movie: Ponyo, 2008

Cook: Lisa


Click to see more


Lisa prepares two bowls of ramen for Sosuke and Ponyo dinner. These bowls seem like simple instant noodles at first, but with the addition of some extra ingredients they turn into rich and flavorful Ramen Noodles. Careful, after eating a full bowl you might fall asleep, just like Ponyo did in the movie.

Ingredients


  1. 1 instant ramen noodle packet

  2. 1 egg

  3. 1 green onion

  4. 1 piece ham

  5. 2.5 cups water

Instructions


  1. Soft boil the egg and cut in half, slice the green onions, and cut the ham in half. This is a personal preference but cook the ham a bit.

  2. Boil water in a kettle or on the stove.

  3. Place the ramen noodles in a bowl. If your ramen comes with a seasoning packet, throw it away. NOOOO, Iâ€™m joking, if you want to add your flavor to this ramen add the packet to the bowl, if you want the OG experience, STORE the packet. No waste allowed in my Picnic.

  4. Pour the boiling water into the bowl where the ramen is. Cover the bowl and let it sit according to the ramen package instructions, normally 3 to 5 minutes.

  5. Once cooked, uncover the bowl and separate the noodles using chopsticks or a fork. Mix the combination a bit.

  6. Top with half of the egg, sliced green onion, and both pieces of cut ham.

  7. Enjoy immediately so the noodles don't get soggy!
