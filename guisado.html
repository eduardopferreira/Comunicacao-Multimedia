Sheeta's Beef Stew


Sheeta cooks this beef stew for the air pirates dinner. Amazed by her process the manly Pirates, who start by observing the cooking, end up helping her in the kitchen. This joyful moment brings the crew together, just as we it does bring at your house.

Ingredients


  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 to 1.5 lb stewing beef, cut into chunks
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 medium onion
  • Oil for browning
  • 1/4 cup red wine
  • 1/2 can tomatoes (14 oz) OR 4 fresh tomatoes pureed (1.75 cup)
  • 1 tsp basil
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 Tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 1/4 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 1 1/2 potatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • Instructions


    1. 1. In a large bowl, season the beef stew meat with salt, pepper, and flour. Toss to coat evenly.

    2. 2. Heat the olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Add the beef in batches and brown on all sides. Remove the beef from the pot and set aside.

    3. 3. In the same pot, add the chopped onion and garlic. Saut√© until softened, about 5 minutes.

    4. 4. Return the beef to the pot. Add the beef broth, red wine (if using), bay leaves, and thyme. Bring to a simmer.

    5. 5. Cover the pot and let it simmer for about 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the beef is tender.

    6. 6. Add the carrots and potatoes to the pot. Simmer for an additional 30 minutes, or until the vegetables are cooked through.

    7. 7. If using frozen peas, add them to the pot in the last 5 minutes of cooking.

    8. 8. Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.

    9. 9. Remove the bay leaves before serving.

    10. 10. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley if desired.
