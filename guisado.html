Sheeta's Beef Stew


Movie: Castle in the Sky, 1986

Cook: Sheeta


Sheeta cooks this Vegetable soup for the air pirates dinner. Amazed by her process the manly Pirates, who start by observing the cook, end up helping her in the kitchen. This joyful moment brings the crew together, just as we hope it does at your house.

Ingredients


  1. 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  2. 1/2 tsp. salt
  3. 1/4 tsp pepper
  4. 1/2 tsp paprika
  5. 1 to 1.5 lb stewing beef, cut into chunks
  6. 2 garlic cloves
  7. 1 medium onion
  8. Oil for browning
  9. 1/4 cup red wine
  10. 1/2 can tomatoes (14 oz) OR 4 fresh tomatoes pureed (1.75 cup)
  11. 1 tsp basil
  12. 1 tsp oregano
  13. 1 Tbsp brown sugar
  14. 1 1/4 carrots, peeled and chopped
  15. 1 1/2 potatoes
  16. Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions


  1. For this recipe, don’t forget to use your oldest pot.

  2. First season your meat with salt, pepper, and flour. Coat evenly.

  3. Heat your olive oil over medium heat in the pot. Add the meat until all sides are brown, then remove it and set it aside.

  4. The onion dives in second, to be softened and caramelized for about 5 minutes.

  5. Then, add the garlic to cook for another minute. Careful! Your kitchen will be filled with a rich aroma…

  6. Return the beef to the pot. Add the red wine, beef broth, bay leaves, and thyme.

  7. Stirring occasionally, bring to a simmer

  8. Close the pot and let it simmer on LOW HEAT for about 1.5 hours.

  9. Add your carrots and potatoes, already cut in small pieces, let them cook for another 30 minutes or until cooked.

  10. Taste your soup, it's already finished. Now you just need to adjust the seasoning, take the bay leaves and serve.
