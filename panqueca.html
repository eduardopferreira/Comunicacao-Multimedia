Kiki's Pancakes


Movie: Kiki's Delivery Service, 1989

Cook: Kiki


These pancakes were eaten by Kiki and Jiji during the movie. Although this was not a major scene the pancakes look appetizing and would have a special spot at our picnic table.

Ingredients


  1. 1 cup cake flour

  2. 1 tsp baking powder

  3. Pinch of salt

  4. 1 egg, room temperature

  5. 2 Tbsp sugar

  6. 1/2 cup buttermilk, room temperature

  7. 1 Tbsp melted butter

  8. 1 tsp vanilla

  9. 1 tsp vegetable or canola oil

    10. Makes 4 pancakes


Instructions


  1. Mix the dry ingredients first.

  2. In a separate bowl, combine the wet ingredients. I guess these are self-explanatory.

  3. Make a hole in the dry ingredient mix and pour in the wet ingredients.

  4. Stir until barely combined. Lumps are fine!

  5. Allow rising for 3 to 5 minutes.

  6. Heat a nonstick pan over medium heat.

  7. Drop about 1/3 cup of batter into the pan.

  8. Cook one side until the pancake bubbles and slides around easily.

  9. Flip and cook the opposite side.

  10. Place the cooked pancake on a plate and add a square of butter on top, so it can melt and look cool! Never spread your butter.

  11. Ok, now for the unique part, you will add two cherry tomatoes and half a sausage to that plate.
